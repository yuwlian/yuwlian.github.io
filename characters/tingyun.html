Tingyun

Harmony | Lightning | Main Menu
Lightcone
  • Meshing Cogs
    • Very accessible, and amazing energy gains.
    • Allows 3T ult with skill once basic attack twice, assuming 24.4%* ERR.
    • Allows 3T ult with only basic attack, assuming 24.4%* ERR and get hit once.
    • *Needs Penacony or Vonwacq.
  • But the Battle Isn't Over
    • Increases ERR% and gives 1 SP every 3 ult.
    • Needs speed tuning to make use of the dmg buff.
  • Planetary Rendezvous
    • Best option when Tingyun is paired with a lightning dps.
  • Dance! Dance! Dance!
    • Doesn't need superimpositions and nice advance forward for the whole team.
  • Carve the Moon, Weave the Clouds
    • Bad at low superimpositions, not even decent at S5. Not recommended.
Relic
  • 4P Hacker
    • Increases spd, and the spd of the ally Tingyun uses ult on.
  • 4P Musketeer
    • Increases atk, spd, and basic attack dmg.
  • 2P Musketeer + 2P Hacker
    • Increases atk and spd.
  • 2P Long + 2P Hacker
    • Increases hp and spd.
Planar
  • 2P Broken Keel
    • Increases effect res and gives crit dmg for the whole party.
  • 2P Fleet
    • Increases hp and boosts atk for the whole party.
  • 2P Penacony
    • 5% ERR + 10% lightning* dmg boost to other allies.
    • *Depends on the wearer's element.
  • 2P Vonwacq
    • 5% ERR + 40% advance forward when entering battle.
Stats & Skill Priority
  • Main Stat
    • Boots - SPD
    • Body - HP%
    • Sphere - HP%
    • Rope - Energy%
  • Sub Stat
    • SPD > HP% > ATK% > RES%
  • Skill
    • Ult > Skill > Talent > Basic Attack
